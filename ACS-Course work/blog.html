Application for Plant Age identification

October 05, 2024

Problem outline
In this era there are no simple solutions to identify the age of the plants at a simple application.with a one click of the uploaded of the plants photos. This innovation might outline the solution for that

Solution for the realtime problem
Plant Age Deductor is a system that uses hardware sensors and AI-based software to analyze various biological and environmental factors of a plant to estimate its age

To solve this problem it involves the hardware and software part.
Hardware : The system consists of a camera, temperature sensor, humidity sensor, and light sensor to collect data from the plant's environment.


Conclusion
The Plant Age Deductor will revolutionize the way we monitor and manage plant growth. It will provide valuable insights to farmers, gardeners, and researchers to optimize plant care and improve crop yield. The system will also benefit the environment by promoting sustainable agriculture practices and reducing food waste.
